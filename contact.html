The Badoc Farmers Multipurpose Center is a vital hub for the local agricultural community in Badoc, Ilocos Norte, Philippines. It serves as a venue for various activities such as training, seminars, and workshops aimed at enhancing the knowledge and skills of farmers in modern agricultural practices, sustainable farming methods, and the use of new technologies in agriculture.
